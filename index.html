Dental veneers are thin, custom-made shells that cover the front surface of teeth to improve appearance. Made from porcelain or composite resin, they can fix discoloration, chips, gaps, or misshapen teeth. With proper care, porcelain veneers typically last 10-15 years.

Yes, professional teeth whitening is completely safe when performed by licensed dental professionals. Our whitening treatments use FDA-approved materials and are customized to minimize sensitivity while delivering dramatic results - up to 8 shades brighter in just one visit.

The dental implant process typically involves three stages: 1) Initial consultation and 3D imaging, 2) Surgical placement of the titanium implant (which integrates with your jawbone over 3-6 months), and 3) Placement of the custom crown. The entire process usually takes 3-6 months, resulting in a permanent, natural-looking tooth replacement.

Absolutely! We offer same-day emergency appointments for urgent dental issues like severe pain, broken teeth, or knocked-out teeth. Call us immediately if you have a dental emergency - we'll do everything possible to see you the same day.

Clear aligners are removable, transparent trays that gradually straighten teeth. Unlike traditional braces, they're virtually invisible, can be removed for eating and cleaning, and typically require fewer office visits. Treatment time is comparable to braces (12-18 months on average) and they're effective for most orthodontic issues.
